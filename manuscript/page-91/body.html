Spiritual Commentary:

Having received kriya from the guru and by regular practice one realizes the kutastha and the essence of consciousness. This is called knowledge. To look outward with attachment is ignorance. Asakta buddhih sarvatra jitatma vigata sprihah Naishkarmya siddhim paramaam sannyasena adhigacchati. 22 (18/49)

Translation:

Sarvatra (everywhere) asakta buddhih (being unattached) jitatma (having control of mind) vigata sprihah (without material desire) sannyasena (by renounced order of life) paramaam (supreme) naishkarmya siddhim (the perfection of non reaction) adhi gacchati (attains) One who is self-controlled and unattached and who disregards all material enjoyment
