(destroyer of worldly pains) bhavati (becomes)

He who is regulated in his habits of eating, sleeping, recreation and work can mitigate all worldly pains by practicing yoga system.

Spiritual Commentary:

One should eat, move, sleep, make efforts and work by remaining in the after effect poise of kriya. Otherwise you will not succeed in yoga practice. If you follow the disciplines you will not face difficulty.

Yam labdhva chaparam labhdam manyate nadhikam tatah Yasmin sthito na duhkhena gurunapi vichalyate (6/22)

Translation:

yam (which) cha (and) labdhva (by attainment) tatah (than that) adhikam (superior) aparam (any other) labham (acquistion) na (never) manyate (considers) na (not) adhikam(greater) tatah (that) yasmin (in which) sthito (being situated ) na (not) dukkhena (sorrow) guruna (very difficult) api (even though) vichalyate
