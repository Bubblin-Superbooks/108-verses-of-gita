Yukteshwar Giri. Disciple of Mahavatar Sri Sri Babaji Maharaj and guru of Sri Yukteshwar Giriji.

He was a family man with miraculous powers. His mission was to make Kriya Yoga known to the modern man for striking a balance between his worldly duties and spiritual meditation.

Sri Sri Swami Sri Yukteshwar Giri (1855-1936)
