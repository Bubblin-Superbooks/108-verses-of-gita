Spiritual Commentary:

Stay put at the after-effect poise of kriya and give up desire for good or bad actions. Holding on to the after-effect poise of kriya perform all actions. The practice of kriya yoga is quite difficult and technical. For example, you will have to put the eyes on the head and keep the breath at chest and perform all your actions.
