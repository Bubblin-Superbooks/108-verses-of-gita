Spiritual Commentary:

The spiritual science is true science of knowledge. Kriya yoga is real science which is found after a great deal of effort. When one remains perfectly attuned with the knowledge of the Self, then one is said to be in the state of yoga. Therefore, one should receive instructions from his guru and practice kriya without entertaining any desire for anything. Sarvabhutastham atmanam sarvabhutani chatmani Iksate yogayuktatma sarvatra samadarshanah 21 (6/29)

Translation:

yoga yuktatma (one who is united to spirit by yoga) sarvatra (everywhere) samadarsanah (seeing equally) atamanam (the super soul) sarva bhutani (all beings) cha (and) sarvabhutastham (situated in all beings)
