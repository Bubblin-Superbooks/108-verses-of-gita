Spiritual Commentary:

Having made pleasure and pain, gain and loss the same, practice kriya. Then you will attain Brahma. Yogasthah kuru karmaani sangam tyaktva Dhananjaya Siddhi-asiddhyoh samo bhutva samatvam yoga uchyate (2/48)

Translation:

Dhananjaya (O Arjuna) yogasthah (equipoised in yoga) sangam (attachment) tyaktva (giving up) siddhyasiddhyo (in success of failure) samah
