Spiritual Commentary:

He who at the time of death, renounces his body remembering Me at the kutastha, attains My nature as because his mind remained always fixed on Me. He attains freedom. There is no doubt about it. A kriyaban experiences dissolution every day. Tasmat sarveshu kaleshu mam anusmara yudhya cha Mayi arpita-mano-buddhih mam eva aishysi asamashayam (8/7)

Translation:

tasmat (therefore) sarveshu (all) kaleshu (times) mam (me) anusmara (go on remembering) yudhya (fight) cha (and) mayi (unto me) arpita (surrendering) manah (mind) buddhih (intellect) mam (unto me) eva (certainly) asamsayam (without doubt) aisyasi (you will attain) Therefore, you (Arjuna) should think of Me and at the same time carry out your prescribed duty of
