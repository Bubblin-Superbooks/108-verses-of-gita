Translation:

manmanah (with mind fixed on me) bhava (be) madbhaktah (devoted to me) madyaji (worship me) mam (me) namaskuru (bow down) mam eva (me only) eshyasi (you will come) te (you) priyo (dear) asi (you are) pratijane (I promise) Fix your mind on Me, be devoted to Me, worship Me, prostrate before Me so that you will come to Me without fail. This is My promise for you are dear to Me.

Spiritual Commentary:

Fix your mind on Me, that is, practice kriya. Bow down to Me, means practice omkar kriya which is to be learnt from the guru. I give you assurance that you will become My own. I promise you because you are dear to Me. Sarvadharman parityajya mam ekam sharanam Vraja Aham tva sarva papebhyo mokshayishyami ma suchah (18/66)
