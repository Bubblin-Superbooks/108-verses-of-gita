Translation:

atmana (wisdom united mind) atmanam (the conditioned soul) uddhared (one must deliver) avasadayet (put into degradation) hi (indeed) atma (ego/mind) eva (certainly) atmanah (of the soul) bondhuh (friend) atma eva (certainly the ego/mind) ripuh (enemy) atmanah (of the soul) One must deliver himself with the help of his wisdom united mind and not degrade himself. The mind is the friend of the conditioned soul, and his enemy as well.
