Spiritual Commentary:

Brahma is the doer of all actions. He is the dispenser, he remains self-absorbed. This is called tapasya or meditation i.e. holding onto the kutastha. He is in all and in all individuals. Though He is separate, yet He is the Supreme Lord residing in the heart of all beings in the form of breath (prana, the life force).
