Translation:

shatrau (to an enemy) mitra cha (also to a friend) tatha manapamanayoh (so in honour and dishonour) samah (equal) shitoshna-sukha-duhkheshu (in cold and heat, in happiness and distress) samah (equipoised) sanga vivarjitah (free from all association). One who is equal to friends and enemies, who is equipoised in honour and dishonour, heat and cold, happiness and distress, fame and infamy, is always free from contaminating association.

Spiritual Commentary:

Absorbed in after effect poise of kriya he just like a drunkard person, treats friend or foe, honour and dishonour, cold and warm, pain and pleasure all alike. He entertains no desire for anything whatsoever. Tulya-ninda-stutih mauni santushto yena kenachit Aniketah sthira-matih bhaktiman me priyo narah. (12/19)
