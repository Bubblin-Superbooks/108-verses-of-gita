(also) nityam (constant) samachittatvam (equilibrium) Remain detached and free from entanglement with children, wife, home and the rest always mentally balanced in pleasant and unpleasant events.

Spiritual Commentary:

He is detached internally towards his wife, children and home. He treats good and bad alike. Mayi chanaya yogena bhaktih avyabhicharini Vivikta-desha-sevitvam aratih jana samsadi 20 (13/11)

Translation:

mayi (unto me) cha (also) ananya yogena (by analloyed devotional service) avyabhicharini (unswerving) bhakti (devotion) vivikta desha sevitam (staying in a solitary place) jana samsadi (general mass of people) aratih (being without attachment) Constant and unalloyed devotion to Me, aspiring to live in a solitary place, detachment from general mass of people.
