Yuktaharaviharasya yukta chestasya karmasu Yukta svapnavabodhasya yogo bhavati duhkhaha (6/17)

Translation:

yukta (regulated) ahara (eating) viharasya (recreation) karmasu (in discharging duties) yukta (regulated) chestasya (of one who works for maintenance) yogah (practice of yoga) dukhaha (destroyer of worldly pains) bhavati (becomes) He who is regulated in his habits of eating, sleeping, recreation and work can mitigate all worldly pains by practicing yoga system.
