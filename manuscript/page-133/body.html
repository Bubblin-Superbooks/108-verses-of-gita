Arjuna, become a yogi.

Spiritual Commentary:

To remain in the after effect poise of kriya is greater than concentration in between the eyebrows. It is greater than practice of yonimudra and even better than kriya practice. So be a yogi. Yoginam api sarvesham mad gatena antaratmana Shraddhavan bhajate yo mam sa me yuktatamo matah. (6/47)

Translation:

yo (one who) shradhavan (devotee with full faith) mat gatena (abiding in me, thinking of me) mam (to me) bhajate (renders transcendental service lovingly) sarvesham (of all types) yoginam api (of yogis also) sa (he) yuktatmo (the greatest yogi) me (my) matah (opinion). Out of all yogis, the one with great faith who always abides in Me, thinks of Me within himself and renders transcendental service lovingly to Me, is the most intimately united with Me in yoga.
