Spiritual Commentary:

The senses snatch away the mind of even a disciplined and controlled man. Tani sarvaani samyamya yukta asita matparah Vashe hi yasya indriyani tasya prajna pratishthita 7 (2/61)

Translation:

tani (those) sarvaani (all senses) samyamya (keeping under control) matparah (in relationship with me) yukta (engaged) asita (should be situated ) hi (certainly) yasya (one whose) indriyani (senses) vashe (in full subjugation) tasya (his) prajna (wisdom consciousness) pratishthita (fixed) One who restrains his senses, keeping them under full control and fixed his consciousness upon Me is known as a man of steady wisdom.
